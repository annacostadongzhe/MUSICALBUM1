# 松声 · Whispering Pines
## 尺八冥想专辑 —— 12 首曲目 Suno 制作方案

专辑概念：清晨海边松林中的尺八冥想之旅。每首曲目对应松林一日之晨的不同瞬间。
混入元素：尺八、人声（呼吸/诵念/泛音）、松涛、海浪、晨鸟、露水

---

## Track 01 · 晨雾 (Morning Mist) — 2:30

**意境**：黎明前的海边松林，雾气尚未散去。万物未醒，只有最轻的呼吸。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Zen meditation, ambient, shakuhachi, field recording, Japanese minimalism
> A very sparse shakuhachi piece with extended breath sounds. One or two notes every 30 seconds. Soft wind through pine needles in background. Distant ocean waves barely audible. Long silences. No beat, no melody, just atmosphere and space.
> Male low humming ohm fades in and out like a distant temple bell.
> Gradually build from near-silence to a gentle presence.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：冥想氛围，禅宗，尺八，自然环境录音
> 极简的尺八乐曲，大量留白。每30秒只出现一两个音符。背景是松林中的微风声，远方海浪几乎不可闻。长时间的静默。没有节拍，没有旋律，只有氛围和空间。低沉的男声嗡鸣如远处寺庙钟声般若隐若现。从几乎无声逐渐发展到轻柔的存在感。

关键词 (Tags): shakuhachi, meditation, field recording, wind, ambient, sparse, breath, zen

## Track 02 · 松涛 (Pine Waves) — 3:00

**意境**：风吹过松林，松针的声音像海浪一样起伏。尺八在风中与松涛对话。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Meditative ambient, Japanese flute, nature immersion
> Wind sweeping through a pine forest, creating a wave-like rustling texture. Shakuhachi plays slow, breathy phrases that rise and fall like the wind. The flute and the wind are in conversation. Very organic, almost like the shakuhachi IS the wind. Subtle low ohrn drone anchors the piece. The sound of pine needles shifting. No rhythm, no percussion.
> Use dynamic swells: wind builds, shakuhachi responds, then fades back.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：冥想氛围，日本尺八，自然沉浸
> 风吹过松林，松针的沙沙声像海浪一样起伏。尺八吹出缓慢而充满气息的乐句，随着风声起伏。笛声与风声在对谈。非常有机，尺八几乎化作风本身。低沉嗡鸣作为锚点。松针摇曳的声音。没有节奏，没有打击乐。动态起伏：风声渐强，尺八回应，再渐渐消退。

关键词 (Tags): shakuhachi, wind, pine forest, ambient drone, nature, breathy, dynamic swells, meditation

## Track 03 · 潮音 (Tide Tone) — 3:30

**意境**：潮水的声音成为整首曲子的节奏。尺八像从海水深处浮现的旋律。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Ambient, water sounds, shakuhachi meditation, deep listening
> Ocean waves washing onto a sandy shore as the primary rhythmic element. Shakuhachi notes emerge from the water texture, long and deep, like sounds rising from the ocean floor. Each phrase connected to the rhythm of the tide. Subtle male throat chanting deep and low, almost like a foghorn. The piece breathes with the ocean.
> Slow, deep, grounded. No high frequencies, mostly low-mid range.
> A single shakuhachi note holds and bends as a wave crashes.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：氛围音乐，海浪声，尺八冥想，深度聆听
> 海浪拍打沙滩的声音作为主要节奏元素。尺八音符从水的纹理中浮现，悠长而深沉，像是从海底升起的声音。每个乐句都与潮汐的节奏相连。深沉的男性喉音诵念，低沉如雾号。整首曲子随海洋呼吸。
> 缓慢、深沉、扎根。没有高频，主要是中低频。
> 一个尺八音符随着波浪拍打而延续和弯曲。

关键词 (Tags): shakuhachi, ocean waves, shore, deep drone, throat singing, low frequencies, grounded, coastal

## Track 04 · 朝露 (Morning Dew) — 2:30

**意境**：第一缕阳光照亮松针上的露珠。透明、轻盈、晶莹。最安静的一首。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Minimalist ambient, crystal, morning, delicate
> The lightest piece in the album. Single water droplet sounds (dew falling from pine needles) spaced 5-10 seconds apart. Shakuhachi in the highest register, fragile and thin, like light touching water. Female ah vocal, barely a whisper, floating. The atmosphere is transparent, like morning air before the world wakes.
> Extremely delicate dynamics. Almost no low end. Silence between sounds is sacred.
> Must feel like the first moment of light.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：极简氛围，水晶般透明，清晨，精致
> 专辑中最轻盈的一首。水滴声（露珠从松针落下）每隔5-10秒出现一次。尺八用最高音区，脆弱而纤细，像光照在水面上。女性啊声吟唱，几近耳语，飘浮着。氛围是透明的，像世界醒来前的清晨空气。
> 极其细腻的动态。几乎没有低频。声音之间的静默是神圣的。
> 必须让人感受到第一缕光的瞬间。

关键词 (Tags): minimalist, delicate, morning dew, water drops, high shakuhachi, female vocal, whisper, transparent

## Track 05 · 光篩 (Light Through) — 3:00

**意境**：阳光透过松枝洒下斑驳光影。温暖开始扩散。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Warm ambient, forest, morning light, gentle
> Morning sunlight filtering through pine branches, creating dappled light patterns on the forest floor. Shakuhachi plays warm, gentle phrases with long sustained notes. Soft golden glow in the texture. Gentle birdsong in distance (one or two calls, not constant). A soft pad like warm air. The feeling of sitting in a patch of sunlight.
> Build from cool to warm over the course of the piece.
> Subtle warmth in the reverb - like the sun heating the air.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：温暖氛围，森林，晨光，温柔
> 晨光透过松枝洒下，在松林地表形成斑驳的光影。尺八吹奏温暖而温柔的乐句，长音符延绵。声音纹理中有柔和的金色光芒。远处的轻柔鸟鸣（一两声鸣叫，不持续）。一个软垫般的音色如暖空气。坐在阳光斑块中的感觉。
> 整首曲子从清凉渐变到温暖。
> 混响中带有微妙的暖意——像阳光加热了空气。

关键词 (Tags): warm ambient, forest, sunlight, dappled light, gentle shakuhachi, birdsong, warm reverb

## Track 06 · 海鳥 (Sea Birds) — 2:30

**意境**：海鸟的叫声划过天空。尺八模仿鸟鸣，与真实的鸟声交织。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Nature musique concrète, shakuhachi, birds, open sky
> Sea birds calling over the ocean and pine forest. Their cries mix with shakuhachi notes that mimic and respond to the birds. The soundscape is open, airy, with a sense of vast space. Wind in the high branches. A single seagull cry triggers a shakuhachi response.
> Alternating between real bird calls and shakuhachi bird-like tones.
> Wide stereo field - birds left, shakuhachi center-right.
> A feeling of the sky opening up above the trees.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：自然具体音乐，尺八，鸟鸣，开阔天空
> 海鸟在海洋和松林上空鸣叫。它们的叫声与尺八模仿和回应鸟鸣的音符交织。声景开阔、空灵，有广阔的空间感。高枝上的风声。一声海鸥鸣叫触发尺八的回应。
> 在真实的鸟鸣和尺八模仿的鸟鸣之间交替。
> 宽阔的立体声场——鸟在左，尺八在右中。
> 森林上方天空打开的感觉。

关键词 (Tags): shakuhachi, sea birds, call and response, open soundscape, wide stereo, wind, sky

## Track 07 · 磐坐 (Sitting Rock) — 3:30

**意境**：一块巨大的岩石坐落在松林中，沉稳、不动。万物流动，唯有此石不移。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Deep meditation, grounded, slow, ritual
> The most meditative track. Deep, slow shakuhachi notes with maximum sustain - each breath is an eternity. Low male chanting (ohm) as a continuous drone. The feeling of sitting on a large rock in the pine forest, not moving. The sea is nearby but distant. Your own breathing becomes the rhythm. The shakuhachi plays as if carved from the rock itself.
> Very slow tempo - one phrase per 20-30 seconds.
> Deep reverb like a natural amphitheater among the pines.
> No urgency, no movement, just presence.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：深度冥想，扎根，缓慢，仪式感
> 最冥想的一首。深沉、缓慢的尺八音符，最大限度的延续——每次呼吸都是一个永恒。低沉的男性诵念（嗡）作为持续的嗡鸣。像坐在松林中一块大石头上，纹丝不动。海在附近但遥远。你自己的呼吸成为节奏。尺八仿佛是从岩石本身雕琢出来的。
> 极慢的速度——每20-30秒一个乐句。
> 深长的混响，如同松林中的天然露天剧场。
> 没有紧迫感，没有移动，只有存在。

关键词 (Tags): deep meditation, grounded, slow ritual, ohm drone, sitting practice, long notes, natural reverb

## Track 08 · 水鏡 (Water Mirror) — 3:00

**意境**：雨后的水洼倒映着天空和松林。水面静止如镜。尺八模拟水面的涟漪。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Ambient, water reflection, still, glassy
> A still water puddle reflecting the pine trees and morning sky above. Shakuhachi notes are like ripples disturbing the reflection - each note creates a momentary shimmer. Subtle water drip sounds. The atmosphere is glassy, fragile. A single held note with subtle pitch bends like light refracting through water.
> The shakuhachi should sound like it's underwater or filtered through water.
> Very clean, pure tones. Minimal reverb, tight space.
> When the shakuhachi stops, the reflection becomes still again.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：氛围音乐，水中倒影，静止，玻璃般
> 一洼静水倒映着松林和天空。尺八音符如同扰乱倒影的涟漪——每个音符都创造出瞬间的闪烁。微妙的水滴声。氛围如玻璃般脆弱易碎。一个持续的长音，带有微妙的音高弯曲，如同光线在水中折射。
> 尺八听起来像是在水下或被水过滤。
> 非常干净、纯粹的音色。最小混响，紧凑的空间。
> 尺八停止时，倒影恢复静止。

关键词 (Tags): ambient, water reflection, still, glassy, clean shakuhachi, pitch bend, water drops, fragile

## Track 09 · 山風 (Mountain Wind) — 3:30

**意境**：风从海面吹向松林，穿过山谷。风中带着海的味道和松的清香。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Wind soundscape, shakuhachi, open landscape
> Wind as the main instrument. Recorded wind through pine trees combined with shakuhachi breath sounds - the line between wind and flute dissolves. The piece moves from the coastline upward through the forest. Dynamic journey: starts with sea breeze (gentle), builds to wind in the crowns (strong), then settles back.
> Shakuhachi uses heavy breath sounds, almost no clear pitch - just the sound of air.
> Wide dynamic range. The feeling of standing in an exposed place, wind all around.
> An overtone singing voice appears like a second wind.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：风声声景，尺八，开阔景观
> 风是主要乐器。松林中的风声录音与尺八的气息声融合——风与笛的界限消融。音乐从海岸线向上穿过森林。动态旅程：从海风（轻柔）开始，发展到树冠中的风（强烈），然后回归平静。
> 尺八使用厚重的气息音，几乎没有明确的音高——只有空气的声音。
> 宽阔的动态范围。站在开阔之地的感觉，四周都是风。
> 泛音唱法的声音出现，如同第二阵风。

关键词 (Tags): wind soundscape, shakuhachi breath, overtone singing, dynamic journey, open landscape, air sounds

Suno Prompt (English):

> Focus on low-mid frequencies. Warm, dark, enveloping.
> The shakuhachi plays like it's rooted in the ground.
> Subtle crackling sounds (pine cones, twigs, dried needles underfoot).

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：大地氛围，地面，有机，深度聆听
> 森林地面。低频、丰富而深沉。松针上的脚步声。深沉到在胸腔中共鸣的尺八音符。男性低沉到成为大地一部分的哼鸣。苔藓、土壤、根系。这首曲子闻起来像雨后泥土的气息。
> 聚焦中低频。温暖、深沉、包裹。

## Track 11 · 暮れ凪 (Evening Calm) — 3:30

**意境**：一天即将结束，海面平静无风。最后的温暖光线。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Evening ambient, sunset, calm, closing
> The calm before dusk. The sea is flat, almost no wind. The last warm light of the day filters through the pines. Shakuhachi plays long, fading notes - each one feels like it might be the last. A female ah vocal, soft and distant. The piece gradually reduces to almost nothing.
> Decrescendo throughout the entire piece. Starts gentle, ends in near-silence.
> Warm, golden, nostalgic. The feeling of a day well spent.
> The final note should feel like a held breath before sleep.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：傍晚氛围，日落，平静，闭幕
> 黄昏前的平静。海面如镜，几乎没有风。一天最后的暖光透过松林。尺八吹奏悠长渐弱的音符——每一个都感觉可能是最后一个。轻柔而遥远的女性啊声吟唱。整首曲子逐渐减少到几乎无声。
> 全曲渐弱。开始温柔，结束于近乎无声。
> 温暖、金色、怀旧。一天充实度过后的感觉。
> 最后一个音符应该像入睡前屏住的一口气。

关键词 (Tags): evening ambient, sunset, decrescendo, last light, fading, nostalgic, calm, closing

## Track 12 · 松籟 (Pine Resonance) — 4:00

**意境**：整张专辑的核心——松林本身的声音。尺八、人声、自然声的终极融合。

Suno Prompt (English):
> Style: Meditation finale, full immersion, pine forest sound
> The album's centerpiece and finale. All elements come together: shakuhachi, male ohm chanting, female ah, wind, distant waves, birds, pine needles. A slow, 4-minute meditation journey. Starts with all elements present but sparse, builds to a gentle fullness, then releases into silence.
> The shakuhachi plays a simple, memorable phrase - the album's "theme" - that repeats twice, slowly.
> A choir of voices (ohm) swells like the forest itself singing.
> At 3:00, everything drops away except a single shakuhachi note held to fade.
> Silence for the last 15 seconds. The listener hears their own space.

Suno Prompt (中文):
> 风格：冥想终曲，全沉浸，松林之声
> 专辑的核心与终曲。所有元素汇聚：尺八、男性嗡诵念、女性啊吟唱、风、远方海浪、鸟鸣、松针。一段缓慢的4分钟冥想旅程。从所有元素都出现但稀疏开始，发展到温柔的饱满，然后释放进入寂静。
> 尺八演奏一个简单而难忘的乐句——专辑的"主题"——缓慢重复两次。
> 一群声音（嗡）像森林本身在歌唱一样膨胀。
> 3:00处，一切消退，只剩一个尺八音符持续到消失。
> 最后15秒的静默。听众听到自己的空间。

关键词 (Tags): meditation finale, full immersion, choir, forest singing, shakuhachi theme, slow build, release into silence

## Suno 制作建议

1. **每次只生成一首**，用对应的 Prompt，不要同时生成多首
2. 每首生成 2-4 个版本，选出最好的
3. 关键参数：Instrumental 优先（如果不需要人声词），Style 部分用上面的英文 Prompt
4. 生成后用剪辑软件拼接，曲目之间留 3-5 秒静默或淡入淡出
5. 最后一首《松籁》的结尾 15 秒静默可以保留，作为整张专辑的呼吸空间
6. 如果 Suno 生成了歌词，将其视为"器乐人声"处理——没有实际语义的吟唱更符合专辑概念

## 每次生成的步骤

1. 在 Suno 输入 Style 和 Prompt（用英文版）
2. 生成后试听，选最符合意境的版本
3. 下载 WAV/MP3
4. 按序号保存到 generations/v1/
