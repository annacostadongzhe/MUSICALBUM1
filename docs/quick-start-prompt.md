# 尺八冥想音乐 · AI 生成 Prompt 快速启动

如果你有 Suno / Udio / Stable Audio，可以直接用以下 Prompt 来生成核心素材。

---

## Suno 通用 Prompt（中文版）

> [风格：冥想氛围 / 禅宗尺八 / 极简主义]
> [乐器：尺八，低沉人声诵念，自然声景]
> [氛围：迷雾竹林，清晨溪流，微风，远山寺庙]
> 缓慢、空旷、留白充足的尺八冥想曲，尺八气息声与呼吸交替出现，背景有咕咕溪水声和远处鸟鸣。极少的旋律，大量的空间感。长约 3-4 分钟。

## Suno 英文版 Prompt

> Style: Zen meditation, shakuhachi, deep minimalism, ritual ambient, Japanese bamboo forest
> Instruments: shakuhachi flute, subtle overtone singing, field recording of stream and birds
> Atmosphere: misty valley, morning light through bamboo, temple far away
> A slow, breath-centric meditation piece with long silences between notes, shakuhachi breath noise blending with human breathing, gentle water stream bed, very sparse and organic. No beat, no percussion.

---

## Udio 专用 Prompt

> Style: Ambient, meditative, zen, Japanese traditional, nature recording
> A very sparse shakuhachi meditation with extended breath sounds, subtle male low chanting (ohm), running stream water and soft wind through bamboo leaves. Extremely minimal melody. Organic, raw, unhurried. No drums, no rhythm.

---

## 生成技巧

1. **先做 Drone（铺底）**：用"low male ohm drone, bamboo forest atmosphere" 生成一段长氛围铺底
2. **再做尺八旋律层**：用"sparse shakuhachi melody, slow breath, solo" 生成单独的尺八段
3. **最后叠加自然音**：用文本 prompt + 自然录音覆盖上去
4. 在 DAW 中将三层混合，做空间和音量自动化
