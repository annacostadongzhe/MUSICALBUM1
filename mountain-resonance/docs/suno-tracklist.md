# 山音 · Mountain Resonance
## 尺八冥想专辑 —— 12 首曲目 Suno 制作方案

专辑概念：深山古寺，从晨雾到暮色。钟声、溪流、雨、诵念。
混入元素：尺八、僧诵、木鱼、钟、风过山谷、雨、溪流

---

## Track 01 · 霧 (Mist) — 3:00

**意境**：清晨山间的雾气，寺庙在雾中若隐若现。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Misty ambient, mountain, shakuhachi, temple
> Morning mist in a mountain valley. A temple bell sounds once, far away, muffled by fog. Shakuhachi emerges from the mist - breathy, distant, searching. The bell resonates for 30 seconds. Mist drifts through the valley. Low male sutra chanting barely audible from the temple.

---

## Track 02 · 鐘 (Bell) — 3:30

**意境**：钟声的完整旅程——敲响、共鸣、消散。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Temple bell, resonance, shakuhachi, ritual
> A single temple bell ceremony. The bell is struck. Shakuhachi plays in the space between the bell's resonances. The bell's sound fills the valley and slowly fades. Another strike. Male chanting joins the resonance. The piece is built entirely around the bell's decay.

---

## Track 03 · 谷 (Valley) — 3:30

**意境**：风穿过山谷的声音，开阔、辽远。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Valley ambient, wind, shakuhachi, open space
> Wind moving through a mountain valley. Wide, spacious. Shakuhachi plays long notes that echo off distant cliffs. The sound bounces and returns. A vast stereo field. The feeling of standing at the edge of a valley, looking across to the other side. Wind is the only constant.

---

## Track 04 · 雨音 (Rain Sound) — 3:00

**意境**：雨打在屋檐和树叶上的声音。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Rain ambient, temple roof, shakuhachi, water
> Rain falling on a temple roof. Each drop has a different voice on different surfaces: tile, leaf, stone, water. Shakuhachi inside the temple, filtered through the rain. Warm, enclosed, peaceful. The rain creates a constant texture. A female ah is like the rain finding its voice.

---

## Track 05 · 木魚 (Wooden Fish) — 3:30

**意境**：木鱼规律的敲击声，成为冥想的节奏锚点。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Ritual ambient, wooden fish, shakuhachi, chanting
> The wooden fish (mokugyo) strikes at a slow, steady rhythm. Shakuhachi weaves between the strikes. Male sutra chanting in deep tones. The most rhythmic piece. The wooden fish is the heartbeat of the temple. Very grounded. Meditation in sound.

---

## Track 06 · 香 (Incense) — 3:00

**意境**：一缕烟从香炉升起，弯曲、飘散、消失。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Incense ambient, smoke, shakuhachi, drifting
> A single line of incense smoke rising in still air. Shakuhachi follows the smoke's path: rising, curling, dispersing. Very slow, delicate. Female ah vocal like the scent of incense filling the room. The piece is about impermanence - watching something beautiful disappear.

---

## Track 07 · 滝 (Waterfall) — 4:00

**意境**：远处瀑布的声音，宏大而恒常。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Waterfall ambient, mountain stream, shakuhachi, power
> A distant waterfall. Its constant sound is the bed of the piece. Shakuhachi emerges from the waterfall's white noise. Low male ohm like the mountain itself. The power of falling water. The piece builds in intensity as if approaching the waterfall, then settles back.

---

## Track 08 · 蝉時雨 (Cicada Rain) — 3:00

**意境**：夏日蝉鸣如雨，密集而喧嚣。生命的声音。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Summer ambient, cicadas, shakuhachi, life force
> Summer cicadas filling the air with their song. The most alive track. Shakuhachi cuts through the cicada chorus like a voice above the crowd. Male chanting strong and present. The feeling of a mountain summer afternoon. Full, rich, vibrant.

---

## Track 09 · 夕焼 (Sunset) — 3:30

**意境**：夕阳染红山谷，僧人开始晚课。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Sunset ambient, evening glow, shakuhachi, chanting
> The sun setting over the valley, painting everything gold and red. Evening chanting begins in the temple. Shakuhachi plays warm, golden tones. A bell sounds once. The day is ending. Peaceful, slightly melancholic. The beauty of something ending.

---

## Track 10 · 星 (Star) — 3:00

**意境**：夜空中的星星开始显现。一颗，然后又一颗。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Starry ambient, night sky, shakuhachi, emergence
> Stars appearing one by one in the mountain sky. High, clear, distant. Shakuhachi in the highest register - each note is a newly visible star. A female ah as the vastness of space. Very clean, open. The sound of looking up.

---

## Track 11 · 月 (Moon) — 3:30

**意境**：山月。比水月更大、更近、更冷。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Mountain moon, night, shakuhachi, clarity
> The moon over the mountain. Larger, closer, colder than over water. Shakuhachi plays with mountain clarity - no haze, just pure tone. Deep male ohm like the mountain holding the moon. The air is thin and cold at this altitude.

---

## Track 12 · 黎明 (Dawn) — 4:00

**意境**：山谷中的黎明。鸟鸣、钟声、新的一天。

Suno Prompt:
> Style: Mountain dawn, awakening, shakuhachi, morning
> The album's finale. First light touches the mountain peaks. Birds begin to sing. A temple bell calls for morning prayer. Shakuhachi plays a rising melody as the light descends the mountain slopes. Everything comes together: birds, bell, chant, flute, stream. The mountain awakens.
> Build from darkness to full morning light. End with a bell.
